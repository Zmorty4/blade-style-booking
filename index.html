Welcome

Classic craft, easy atmosphere.

The Gentlemen's Lounge offers traditional and modern haircuts, beard trims, and sharp finishing details. Inspired by the original Longridge shop, this site keeps the vintage black-and-gold feel while giving visitors a cleaner booking path.

Walk-ins and appointments are welcome Tuesday to Saturday, with multiple barbers cutting and trimming throughout the day.
